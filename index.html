// Understanding the Variables
Temperature ↑ → air less dense → car runs slower → ET ↑ increases
Barometer ↑ → air more dense → car runs faster → ET ↓ decreases
Vapor Pressure ↑ → more moisture → air less dense → ET ↑ increases
Humidity ↑ → more water vapor → air less dense → ET ↑ increases
Default values are based on empirical data from bracket racing at Northern California tracks. Your car may respond differently — use Auto-Tune or adjust manually.
